Kingdom Hearts

Kingdom Hearts is a series of action role-playing games that blends Disney and Final Fantasy characters and worlds in an original storyline. Players control the protagonist Sora, a young boy wielding a Keyblade, as he travels with Donald Duck and Goofy to stop an evil force called the Heartless.

About The Game.

The game mixes characters and settings from both Final Fantasy and Disney franchises.

Main Characters.

Sora - a Keyblade wielder who journeys through Disney and Pixar worlds to fight against the forces of darkness and protect the light

Donald - the Royal Court Magician of Disney Castle and one of the main companions to the protagonist, Sora

Goofy - the Royal Court Magician of Disney Castle and one of the main companions to the protagonist, Sora

Kari - a main character and a key Princess of Heart in the Kingdom Hearts series, known for her role as a childhood friend of Sora and Riku

Riku - Sora's best friend and rival, and a Keyblade Master who wields both light and darkness.

Mickey - Sora's best friend and rival, and a Keyblade Master who wields both light and darkness.

Gameplay Info

The game showcases real-time combat, exploration, and a focus on story-driven adventure.
